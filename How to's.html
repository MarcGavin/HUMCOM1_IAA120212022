How to cook chicken adobo

  • Required Ingredients
    • 1kg Chicken Parts
    • 2 Tbsp Garlic
    • 1pc Onion
    • 4 Tbsp Soy Sauce
    • 2 Tbsp Vinegar
    • 1/2 Tbsp Black Pepper
    • 1/4 cup oil
    • 5pcs bay leaves

  • Preparation
    • Crush the garlic
    • Cut crushed garlic and onion to minced pieces
    • Prepare pan for cooking

  • How To Cook
    1. Heat the pan
    2. Put the oil and wait for it to heat up
    3. Put minced Garlic and Onions then stir fry until its a little bit brown
    4. Put the 1 kg Chicken parts then stir fry until it is mixed with garlic and onion
    5. Put a lid on the pan then wait for it to stir for 15 mins
    6. After 15mins the chicken will produce chicken oil
    7. Put Soy sauce and Black pepper
    8. Put a lid for 10 mins
    9. After 10mins, put vinegar and bay leaves
    10. Lower the fire and let it simmer for 10mins
    11. Your Chicken adobo is now ready to be served!
